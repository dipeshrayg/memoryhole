'Jimothy', a raccoon with short spine syndrome who roams Seattle, gains cult following online
In short:
A raccoon with a rare birth defect has amassed a legion of online fans after a video of it darting across a footpath and running up a set of stairs racked up more than 6.8 million views in just three days.
Nicknamed "Jimothy", the short-spined raccoon is growing more popular by the day as Seattle locals share their own footage of the unusual-looking celebrity.
What's next?
The animal's condition can cause a range of complications, but Jimothy seems to be coping well, according to wildlife experts.
