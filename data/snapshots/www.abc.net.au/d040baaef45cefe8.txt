A man has died in the Adelaide CBD after police say he became unresponsive during an attempted arrest this afternoon.
Police were called to West Terrace just after 4:15pm, following reports a man was behaving in a disorderly manner and damaging vehicles.
Police said they attempted to arrest the man, but he resisted and became unresponsive.
In a statement, SA Police said, "Despite the efforts of police and paramedics who performed CPR, he died at the scene."
A report is being prepared for the state coroner, and the death will be subject to a Police Commissioner's Inquiry.
