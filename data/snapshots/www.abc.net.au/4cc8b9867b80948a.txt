Medical records and personal details stolen in GP network cyber attack
In short:
A network of GP clinics across the country has been hit in a cyber attack, with sensitive medical records and personal information stolen.
Partnered Health, which operates over 50 general practice and skin cancer clinics, has identified more than a dozen locations where it fears patient information has been taken.
What's next?
The company says it is working with government cyber agencies and has sought legal orders to protect the information stolen.
