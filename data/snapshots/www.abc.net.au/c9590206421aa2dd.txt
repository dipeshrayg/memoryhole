Death toll climbs to 82 after flash flooding in India, Afghanistan and Pakistan
In short:
Heavy rainfall that began at the start of the week has decimated communities in India, Pakistan and Afghanistan.
At least 82 people have died from flash flooding caused by the rain; some residents say they "lost everything".
What's next?
Afghanistan's defence ministry said the military had been deployed to the area to support the relief effort, while the United Nations said it was assessing the disaster.
