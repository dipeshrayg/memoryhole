Kalgoorlie street closed as police investigate woman with stab injuries
Mon 20 Jul 2026 at 12:13pm
, updatedMon 20 Jul 2026 at 12:36pm
Police have closed parts of Kalgoorlie's main street after a woman was found with stab wounds in the early hours of this morning.
Detectives and forensic officers are at the scene on Hannan Street, and members of the public are being told to keep back.
Police responded to reports a woman, aged in her 40s, had been stabbed at 3:40am.
She was taken to Kalgoorlie Health Campus with critical injuries.
The ABC has asked the WA Country Health Service for an update on her condition.
"Detectives believe both parties are known to each other and are treating this incident as targeted, with no ongoing risk to public safety," police said in a statement.
Anyone with any information or footage regarding this incident is urged to contact Crime Stoppers online via www.crimestopperswa.com.au or by calling 1800 333 000.
