Live: Iran Revolutionary Guards say they hit US radar and air defence systems in Bahrain and Kuwait
Iran's Revolutionary Guards said on Tuesday they hit US military targets including air defence systems and a radar installation in Bahrain and Kuwait, state news agency IRNA reported. The US earlier said it had struck Iranian military command centres overnight. Follow our liveblog for the latest developments.
